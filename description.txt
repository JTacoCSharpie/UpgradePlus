UpgradePlus is an upgrade mod based on
Oempa's Upgrade Equipment

The Blacksmith is a town NPC that can arrive after 
EoC, EoW/BoC, or skeletron are defeated. Fight 
bosses and strong mobs for tokens you can trade to 
upgrade your gear. Take your favorite weapons deeper
into the game or grind past difficult bosses.


--Features--
>Upgrade weapons, wings, & wearables
>Stacks with Oempa's EU & hrr_'s fork
>New stats: Crit Damage & Wing Speed

--Improvements--
Right click to max out an item!
Remembers item favorite status
More configs! +Jank test features
Supports all damage types, I think

--Q&A--
* What drops tokens?
Any Boss or NPC above 1k HP drops tokens based on
maxHP+Atk+Def. Boosted in HM + further in postML.

--Known Issues--
* Calamity removes levels when reforging (goblin).
Reforged gear will have it's tokens refunded at 
standard rates (90% returned) to prevent mishaps.
 * Player projectiles spawned from non-hotbar sources
can have wrong Crit Damage and Rollover. 1.3 limit, 
Crit Damage is disabled (config) by default until 1.4
* Crit chance doesn't raise projectile crit chance
* The upgrade slot shows a 1 in the corner
* Wings don't have appropriate speeds in WingSlot 
 or in the social slot using Antisocial
